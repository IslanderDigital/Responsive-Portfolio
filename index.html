About Me

Keith Meunier is a business manager within a family-owned holding company called La Famille Meunier.

Although he is involved with the general administration of La Famille Meunier, currently, Keith is directly responsible for the day-to-day running of Private Facilities, a facilities services company. Private Facilities work closely with building managers and owners by offering core facility services ranging from building maintenance, operations, fire safety, security and cleaning.

Keith has extensive facility management experience spanning over twenty years. He has held national operations positions with major facility service providers such as ISS and Spotless, where Keith was directly responsible for the deployment of services to commercial building including major stadiums in every major Australian city and major events.

His portfolio included clients such as Telstra and Melbourne University, entertainment venues such as ANZ Stadium, Adelaide Stadium, Etihad Stadium (now known as Marvel Stadium), Flemington Racecourse, Royal Randwick and major events such as the Australian Open, Melbourne’s F1 Grand Prix and the Big Day Out and Stereosonic music festivals nationwide.

Within these positions, Keith is very proud to have been involved with the deployment of event services at Melbourne’s Commonwealth Games in 2006 and London’s Olympic Games in 2012.

He brings to Private Services and the broader, La Famille Meunier, transparency, integrity, a focus on team achievement and ground-up operational know-how, through committedly servicing his current clients valuing a highly personalised facilities service provider.

Apart from Private Facilities, Keith is exploring new avenue for La Famille Meunier.

Keith’s special interests are mental health and the fostering of artists which involves him with related organisations which includes on a volunteer basis.

Keith is currently developing his outlook and skills in the digital field to further the family’s opportunities and to benefit his interests.

This online portfolio demostrates his developing skills in the digital field.
